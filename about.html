Snoweria Zhang

Hi! My name is Snoweria. I am an artist + designer who enjoys mathematics, earrings, and tea.

A naturalized Canadian citizen, I have lived and worked in many cities, including Toronto, Boston, Shanghai, Paris, and London. As a result, I am a teensy confused culturally. My work draws inspiration from my travels, daydreams, and actual dreams. I own 43 pairs of earrings, 21 of which I made from things on their way to the trash can. None of them match the dresses I have designed.

In May, 2012, I graduated from Harvard University with B.A. in mathematics and minor in fine arts. Currently, I am completing my masters thesis in architecture at the Harvard Graduate School of Design.

Designer, illustrator, mathematician.
