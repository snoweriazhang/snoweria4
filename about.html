Snoweria Zhang

Hi! My name is Snoweria. I am an artist + designer who enjoys mathematics, earrings, and tea.

A naturalized Canadian citizen, I have lived and worked in many cities. As a result, I am a teensy confused culturally. In 2017, my proposal to hybridize a waste-to-energy plant with a mosque was awarded an Architizer A+ Jury Award. Most recently, my design work was featured on the cover of Nature.

I have a Master of Architecture and a Bachelor of Arts in Mathematics, both from Harvard University. I previouly spent most of my time thinking about future cities at the Senseable City Lab at MIT. Currently, I am the Urban Innovation Architect at KPF.

Below is a list of talks I've given and chapters I've written, in case you really want to know what I think of technology and cities. I also teach sometimes.
